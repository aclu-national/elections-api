What does your county sheriff do?

  • Your sheriff helps shape the county’s priorities so that your community is safe as well as ensures that law enforcement policies promote justice, fairness, and non-discrimination. The county sheriff is usually also responsible for running the local county jail where people charged with crimes might be incarcerated while waiting their day in court.
